Free Fast Track Skill Assessment 2023

It can take a long time for Migration Skills Assessment (MSA) applications to be processed, so Engineers Australia has created a Fast Track Assessment process. Normally, this would take 4 to 7 months, but with the Fast Track Assessment, your application will be granted to an assessor or case officer for preparation within 15 days.

If you are an engineering professional who has migrated to Australia and is looking to boost your job prospects, you may be eligible for a free, expedited skills assessment. This could help meet a critical talent shortage in the country if you possess engineering qualifications or skills and have a permanent family visa, partner visa, refugee visa, or humanitarian visa.

Free Skills Assessment Opportunities for onshore migrants (2022)

The Australian Government has created ‘Incentive 2 – Skills Assessment Opportunities for Migrants’ to aid with the economic recovery of Australia from the effects of COVID-19, as well as to alleviate skills shortages. This incentive is available to migrants in Australia who possess a permanent family, partner, refugee, or humanitarian visa, and have the qualifications, skills, and/or experience in a priority occupation but have not yet had a skills assessment.

Migrants with skills in priority occupations that are not formally recognized can take advantage of the incentive program by receiving a free, quick evaluation of their skills. Those who receive a favorable assessment outcome may be able to find better employment opportunities, thanks to increased trust from employers that they possess the capabilities required to work in Australia.

Eligibility

From 28 February 2022 to 30 June 2023, the Australian Government is offering free, expedited skills assessments to people who meet certain criteria. To be eligible, you must have been granted one of the following visas on or after 1 January 2016:

  • family
  • partner
  • refugee, or humanitarian visa

and you must currently live in Australia. You must also have qualifications or skills in a nominated occupation and have yet to have a formal skills assessment from Engineers Australia or any other assessing authority.

Things You Should Consider for the Assessment

  • Fast Track service can only be applied for after an application has been lodged.
  • To get the Fast Track service, a FAST TRACK service request must be submitted with Migration Skill Assessment Number, Engineers Australia ID, Name, and Payer’s Details.
  • MSA Fast Track service cannot be requested if it was previously applied for at the time of applying.
  • Fast Track service is only available for PRIMARY Applicants; Secondary applications do not require it.
  • Fees for Fast Track service are non-refundable.

Assessment process

After you fill out the form, if you qualify to apply for assessment, you will be directed to the EA portal to complete the application process. Before starting your application, ensure you have all the necessary documents and follow the steps in the migration skills assessment. When you have submitted your application, we will review it and give you an outcome within 15 business days.

  • Process of migration skill assessment:
  • Meet the qualification requirements
  • Understanding engineering occupational categories
  • Choose an assessment pathway
  • Prepare your application
  • Submit your application

How much time would a fast-track assessment take?

Engineers Australia’s Fast Track assessment process can be beneficial in reducing the time it takes for their application to be processed to 20 working days. Although the Outcome of the Migration Skill Assessment is not guaranteed, applicants have a better chance of success if their application is complete and includes all of the required documents and information.

Why Fast Track Skill Assessment?

Applying for a fast-track migration skills assessment strategy is a great way to cut down on the length of time that is typically necessary for normal assessment which takes an average of four to six months. With a fast-track process, it is possible to receive a skills assessment result in as little as two to three weeks. This is a great benefit for those who are looking to reduce their waiting and processing times. Additionally, due to the changing and increasingly stringent regulations regarding migration in Australia, taking advantage of this fast-track option is becoming increasingly important.
